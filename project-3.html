Bloom Skincare

Organic. Minimalist. Beautiful.

Branding Strategy

Bloom is an eco-conscious skincare brand targeting Millennials and Gen Z. The goal was to create a visual identity that felt premium yet approachable, emphasizing the "pure" nature of the ingredients.

Design Direction

I chose a soft pastel palette with earthy undertones. The typography uses a high-contrast serif for headings to convey luxury, paired with a clean sans-serif for body text to maintain modern readability.

Scope

Brand Identity, Logo Design, Packaging
